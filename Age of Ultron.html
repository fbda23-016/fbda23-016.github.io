Advengers Age of Ultron

Synopsis
When Tony Stark (played by Robert Downey Jr.) and Bruce Banner (played by Mark Ruffalo) attempt to jump-start a dormant peacekeeping program called Ultron, things go horribly wrong. Ultron, an artificial intelligence, becomes hostile and vows to achieve “world peace” through mass extinction. Now, Earth’s mightiest heroes must reassemble to stop Ultron from enacting his terrible plan.
